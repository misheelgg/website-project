JKU / Campus / JKU Campus & JKU MED Campus

Get a Taste of Life at the JKU!

Modern laboratories and industrial workshops, a floating café, a 60-meter tall viewing platform, a brand new Learning Center, beach volleyball courts, a soccer field, and much more. The JKU campus is a space to learn and experience new things, relax, and party. Discover the university during a campus tour - either on-site or online!

We would be more than happy to welcome you personally to campus! Just send us an e-mail telling us when you would like to come and how large your group is.

You can also explore the JKU campus from the comfort of your classroom or your own home! You will need Zoom for an online campus tour - click here to learn more about Zoom's data privacy policies .

Regardless of on-site or online, you can get to know Austria's most beautiful campus more up close and personal! We have something for everyone, whether you are interested in pursuing an academic degree at the JKU or you are interested in spending some free time on campus!
